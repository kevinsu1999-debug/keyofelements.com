Foundation I · Yin & Yang

The Two Forces That Shape Everything

一阴一阳之谓道。— Yi Jing, Great Commentary

Before Five Elements, before stems and branches, there is Yin and Yang — the two complementary and inseparable forces whose interplay produces everything that exists. No Yin, no Yang: each only shows itself against the other. Understanding this duality is the first key to reading any BaZi chart.

Yin 阴
Receptive · Still · Inward
Moon · Water · Winter · Night
Yang 阳
Active · Bright · Outward
Sun · Fire · Summer · Day
Foundation II · Five Elements

The Five Elements: Language of All Things

五行：一曰水，二曰火，三曰木，四曰金，五曰土。— Shangshu, Hongfan

Water, Fire, Wood, Metal, Earth — five elemental frequencies that correspond to colors, seasons, organs, emotions, and personality types. They flow between heaven and earth, and through your birth chart. Every Heavenly Stem and Earthly Branch belongs to one of these five.

Generating cycle — each element nourishes the next:

Wood→Fire→Earth→Metal→Water→Wood

Controlling cycle — each element restrains another:

WoodcontrolsEarth·EarthcontrolsWater·WatercontrolsFire·FirecontrolsMetal·MetalcontrolsWood

When Yin-Yang pairs with the Five Elements, the result is the ten Heavenly Stems (甲乙丙丁戊己庚辛壬癸) — the ten Day Masters you see in the cards above.

Foundation III · Four Pillars

BaZi: Eight Characters from Your Birth

知命者不怨天，知己者不怨人。
One who knows destiny does not blame heaven; one who knows the self does not blame others.— Mencius, Jinxin

"BaZi" (八字) literally means "eight characters." Your birth year, month, day, and hour each produce one Heavenly Stem + one Earthly Branch — four pillars of two characters each, eight in total.

The Day Master — the Heavenly Stem of your birth day — represents you. Everything else in the reading is interpreted through its relationship to the Day Master. The ten cards above are the ten possible Day Masters.
