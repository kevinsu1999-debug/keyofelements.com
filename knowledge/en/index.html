The Ten Day Masters

Your Day Master — the Heavenly Stem of your birth day — is the core of every BaZi chart. It sets your temperament, your strengths, and the shape of what a well-lived life looks like for you.

Below are complete classical guides to each of the ten stems, drawn from Ditian Sui, Ziping Zhenquan, and San Ming Tong Hui.

Wood · Yang · The towering tree
乙

Wood · Yin · The flowering vine
丙

Fire · Yang · The sun's light
丁
Yin Fire · Dīng
Fire · Yin · The lantern flame
戊

Earth · Yang · The mountain
己
Yin Earth · Jǐ
Earth · Yin · The cultivated field

Metal · Yang · The axe and blade

Metal · Yin · The jewel and pearl
壬

Water · Yang · The great river
癸
Yin Water · Guǐ
Water · Yin · The morning dew
