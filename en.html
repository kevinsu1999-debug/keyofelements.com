An Ancient Philosophy

For over two thousand years, a philosophy has described the hidden order beneath all things — through the lens of Yin and Yang 阴阳, and the Five Elements 五行. To understand this language is to begin understanding yourself.

Yin & Yang

The Two Forces
That Govern All Things

「一阴一阳之谓道。」
—— 《易经 · 系辞上》

Everything that exists arises from two forces — opposing, yet inseparable. Without Yin 阴, Yang 阳 has no form. Without Yang, Yin has no motion. Together, they are whole.

Yin 阴 is the receptive, the still, the inward — moon, water, winter, night.
Yang 阳 is the active, the radiant, the outward — sun, fire, summer, daylight.
Every person, every color, every season carries a different balance of both.例的混合。

The BaZi Chart:
Your Life Code at the Moment of Birth

「知命者不怨天，知己者不怨人。」

BaZi 八字 — literally, "eight characters." Your year, month, day, and hour of birth each produce one Heavenly Stem 天干 and one Earthly Branch 地支. Four pairs, eight characters. Your life, encoded at the moment you arrived.

The Day Master 日主 is the Heavenly Stem of your Day Pillar — it represents you. Every aspect of the reading radiates outward from this single character.
